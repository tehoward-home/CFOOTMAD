FAQs

  • What is Contra?

    Contra dancing is social, community dancing where everyone is welcome. It's easy to learn, everything is taught, and no dance experience is necessary. There's no need to bring a partner (we typically change partners for each dance of the evening), and all our events feature great live music. Most include a free lesson which is all you'll need for the evening. You can make new friends and have fun in a friendly welcoming crowd. Contra dancing is highly energetic and aerobic.

  • What should I wear?

    wear comfortable clothes and shoes. do not wear shoes with hard black soles that can mar the dance floors.

  • DoI need to bring a partner?

    A partner is not required, you will dance with many partners throughout the evening.

  • Are children welcome?

    yes, children are welcome. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult.
